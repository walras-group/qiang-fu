About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Yale University, advised by Prof. Andre Wibisono. My research lies broadly in optimization, statistics, and machine learning. I am particularly focused on developing provably efficient algorithms for optimization and sampling to improve the training and inference of modern machine learning models. Previously, I received my M.S. and B.S. in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University.
